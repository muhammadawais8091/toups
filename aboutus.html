About Us

Natural Products For Natural Beauty

Wellness begins with what you choose to use on the largest organof your body your skin. All of the products that we use in our nail,skin and body treatments and sell in our expansive retail store have been thoroughly vetted for ingredient content, pureness andefficacy.We search the globe for the newest innovations in green beauty and are committed to partnering with brands who set newstandards for safety in personal care products.

Safe. Non-toxic. Environmentally Conscious

On average,men and women use 10-15 personal care products per day that contain a total of 126 different ingredients. Given that nearly 90% of cosmetic ingredients are untested for safety, there's a lot we don't know about what we are putting on our bodies.

Policing of personal care products is not a priority in the United States. The U.S. has not passed a federal law regulating the ingredients used in personal care products since 1938. Currently, Europe has over 1,300 restricted ingredients, while the U.S. has only 11. (Source: Environmental Working Group

Toups and Co’s mission is to be a resource for natural and eco-friendly products and services that allow our customers to look their best while promoting health and overall wellbeing.

Founder

Emilie Toups

Emilie began Toups and Co Organics not to start a business, but to heal her daughters eczema. “During my search for clean products with ingredients I could trust, I turned to making my own. To my surprise, the hair and skin of my children and I glowed and shined. As I shared products with family and friends, I knew I was on to something. Great skincare shouldn’t only be nourishing and healthy, it needs to WORK. I was blown away with the response to howloved my tallow balms were. How humbling it is to create a product you are not only proud of, but that makes a difference in the lives of others.”

From there Toups and Co grew into a beautiful full-range skincare company and now into Toups and Co Boutique and Spa where youcan find the same attention to ingredients and the same passion for wellness.

The services Toups and Co offers speak to Emilie’s own desires forcleaner and less toxic methods for nails, hair removal, skincare, massage and makeup.
