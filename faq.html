Our makeup and skincare lines are all 100% natural and/or organic and contain no bismuth oxychloride, cornstarch, parabensor sulfates at a minimum. Most are Eco-Cert (learn more here) certified, representing the highest level of organic ingredients and environmentally conscious packaging currently available.At a minimum, each nail polish we carry is free of these six toxic ingredients: formaldehyde, formaldehyde resin, toluene, DBT, TPHP and camphor.

Formaldehyde Commonly used in nail hardeners and nail polishdespite being classified as a known carcinogen by the U.S. National Toxicology Program. It is also used as a tissue fixative and an embalming agent.

Formaldehyde resin: A synthetic polymer used in circuit boards and molded products such as pool balls and coatings and adhesives.

Toluene: An organic solvent with the ability to dissolve paint, rubber, ink, adhesive and lacquers, among other products. It is frequently employed as an octane booster for internal combustion systems

Dibutyl phthalate (DBT): A plasticizer and common additive to adhesives.It is suspected that DBT may disrupt the endocrine system and has been restricted from use in cosmetics by the European Union.

Camphor: Another chemical used in the embalming process, it also has applications as a plasticizer for nitrocellulose and in fireworks. It is highly flammable and toxic even in small doses.

TPHP: Commonly used as a fire retardant in furniture and a hardener in plastic goods, research suggests that the chemical causes changes in hormone regulation, metabolism, and reproductive systems. Ingredient information care of Aila Cosmetics

WHY DO YOU OFFER WATERLESS NAIL SERVICES?

Nails swell in water, which can encourage polish to chip and peel and causes nails to be susceptible to splitting or breaking. Waterless services reduce these inconveniences as well as the risk of complications due to water-borne bacteria and infections.Toups and Co strives to be as eco-friendly as possible, and going waterless means we are doing our part to conserve a valuable resource. Did you know that a typical salon uses 12 gallons of water per pedicure and almost 50,000 gallons per year on all combined services? We are proud to be doing away with that waste.

IF I'M NOT SOAKING, HOW ARE MY HANDS AND FEETCLEANED?

Health and safety are of our utmost concern. We begin all services by spraying each client's hands and feet with a refreshing botanical cleansing spray containing aloe, witch hazel, alcohol, tea tree oil, lavender and vitamin E. This is followed by a warm towel wrap containing all-natural antibacterial essential oils. Our nail consultants wash their hands before every service and apply gloves before servicing each client.
